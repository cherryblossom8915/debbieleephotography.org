OUR MISSION
The Berkshire Business Company is committed to providing small businesses throughout the Berkshires the tools to succeed. Our mission is to promote the success of businesses through their independent works. By providing businesses these tools, we hope to create a community of citizens who embrace and support all businesses within their community.

WEBSITES
Websites are a form of communicating with your audience. Websites are a way for audiences to easily navigate what services you offer, who they can contact, what your prices are, or even get updates on what is happening at headquarters through a blog. These are a tool for people to find information with ease.
LOGOS
Logos are the face of your organization. They often hold a message, some may have objects within their logos, such as compasses which could mean people come from all directions. They could have a landmark that represents who they are. Nevertheless, logos are a way of expanding what your company is about and making it more recognizable at first glance.
BUSINESS CARDS
Business Cards are an essential form of advertising for an organization. Business Cards can be placed at public locations where people are free to take them. You're able to hand them to clients who can then recommend it to their friends earning you a larger base for clientelle. They easily fit into a pocket or wallet making them highly accessible.
INFOGRAPHICS
Infographics are largely important for advertising events or sending a specific message. Infographics can be hung on bulletin boards and can capture your audience's attention more than most other forms of services. Infographics can also be used for social media.
